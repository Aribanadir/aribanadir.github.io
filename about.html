Ariba Nadir - About

About

Ariba Nadir was born on October 13, 1997, in Constantine, into an average family, his father "Samy Ariba" is a doctor who manages the company "Physiopharme", and his mother "Samia Benouar" an art teacher, at the age of 6, the young nadir mastered the basics of computer tools then continued to live normally until the age of 14 when he began to really try programming (HTML, css, Php and MySQL), he then left high school to finish the training of higher technician in Administration and security of computer networks at "INSIM", then the training of administration and security of computer networks near the higher school of computer engineering (ESGI).

After finishing the educational training, Ariba Nadir begins to create his first project "Echrili" an online store, which has failed, then in 2022 he creates the project "Ariba Machines security", which is known to be one of the most powerful monitoring tools in the world and also a rare tool in the computer security market, he then tries other projects that he has stopped by his own choice, such as the creation of soap or a recycling project.

Other Projects that Nadir ariba will finally let see the light of day in 2023 such as the famous "Decimat" project which is essentially a kind of communication box which is also associated with the "Mediteranya" project and also the "Kootob" project based on the artificial intelligence, a set of unique projects, all in Algeria with incredible technological prowess.
